Available Works

Deeply inspired by nature, Nancy Lee's paintings explore the intricate balance of vibrancy and tranquility, reflecting the interconnectedness of all things. She employs a diverse range of media, from acrylics to metallic sheets, using techniques like layering and sculpting to create captivating depth. Discover art where contemporary vision and the natural world converse in beautiful harmony.

About

Discover Nancy Lee, a Filipino-Chinese contemporary and mixed-media artist whose passion for art began at age three, inspired by her artist grandfather. Currently completing her Fine Arts degree, Nancy has already made her mark through children's art workshops, book illustrations, and participation in prestigious competitions like the Shell National Art Competition. Explore Her journey and unique artistic vision.

Exhibits & Features

Nancy Lee's vibrant artistry has earned her significant recognition, including participation in prestigious art competitions like the Shell National Art Competition, Metrobank Art & Design Excellence (MADE), and Sining Filipinas by Zonta. These engagements highlight her growing presence in the art world and the increasing appreciation for her unique artistic vision.
